Best Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

My Family's Famous Recipe

My family is asked to bring these cookies to every church or social gathering because they are just that good! I have been making these with my mom since I was five years old, and now I know the recipe by heart. I hope this recipe can also become one of your family favorites.

Ingredients

    1 1/3 cup Crisco (I like the butter flavored, but either will work)
    1 1/3 cups butter
    2 cups white sugar
    2 cups brown sugar
    4 eggs
    2 tsp vanilla
    6 1/2 cups flour
    2 tsp baking soda
    2 tsp salt
    24 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips (3 cups)

Instructions

  1. Cream together Crisco, butter, and sugars until light and fluffy.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add baking soda, salt, and flour; mix well. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Place balls two inches apart on a greased cookie sheet (I use the medium scoop from Pampered Chef).
  5. Bake for 9-10 minutes at 350. Remove the tray from the oven. Wait two minutes and transfer cookies onto a cookie rack. Enjoy!

Tips:

    This recipe makes a lot of cookies! I frequently cook only 1/2 of the cookies and refrigerate the other half of the dough. That way, when we want hot cookies, I just toss a batch in the oven. The dough is good for as long as the expiration on the eggs.
