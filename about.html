About Me

How I Found My Love For Baking

Hey there! I’m Kara Martinson and I invite you to join me on a delicious journey through the world of baking.

I’m a BYU student-athlete on the Swim and Dive team. When I am not swimming, I am baking. Baking has been my creative outlet for as long as I can remember. From mixing flour and sugar with my mom in the kitchen to experimenting with intricate pastry recipes in the college dorms, baking has always been more than just a hobby for me.

For me, baking is not just about creating sweet treats; it’s about creating memories. I love baking things to share with my friends and family. Each bake tells a story and carries a little piece of my heart with it.

I believe that baking is all about community, and I’d love for you to be a part of ours! Whether you’re an experienced baker or a beginner just starting out, I’m here to support you every step of the way.

Thank you for stopping by, and I can’t wait to embark on this baking adventure with you!

Happy Baking!
